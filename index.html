Frequently Asked Questions

We offer four main types of loans: Personal Loans (up to $50,000), Mortgage Loans (up to $2,000,000), Business Loans (up to $5,000,000), and Student Loans (up to $150,000). Each loan type has specific terms and conditions tailored to its purpose.
Eligibility varies by loan type but generally requires you to be at least 18 years old, have a steady source of income, and meet our credit requirements. Specific loans may have additional requirements (e.g., mortgage loans require property details).
Typically you'll need: Government-issued ID, proof of address (utility bill or bank statement), proof of income (pay stubs or tax returns), and bank account details. Additional documents may be required depending on the loan type.
Most applications receive a decision within 24-72 hours after we receive all required documents. Some simpler loans (like personal loans) may be approved even faster, while complex applications (like large mortgages) may take slightly longer.
Our rates vary by loan type and are based on your credit profile. Current ranges are: Personal Loans from 0.88%, Mortgage Loans from 4.5%, Business Loans with flexible terms, and Student Loans up to 3%. You'll receive your exact rate after application review.
Yes! We encourage early repayment and don't charge prepayment penalties on any of our loans. Early repayment can save you money on interest. Contact us if you'd like to discuss early repayment options.
If you anticipate difficulty making a payment, please contact us immediately. We may be able to arrange alternative payment plans. Late payments may incur fees and could affect your credit score, so we recommend setting up automatic payments to avoid missed due dates.
We use bank-level encryption (256-bit SSL) to protect all data transmissions. Your information is stored securely and only accessed by authorized personnel for legitimate business purposes. We never sell or share your data with third parties for marketing purposes.

Privacy Policy

Data Collection

We collect personal information (name, contact details), financial information (income, employment), and technical data (IP address, cookies) to process your loan application and provide our services.

Data Usage

Your information is used for loan processing, communication, compliance with legal obligations, fraud prevention, and improving our services. We do not sell your data to third parties.

Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures including encryption (SSL/TLS), secure servers, and access controls to protect your data. All employees are trained in data protection best practices.

Your Rights

You have the right to access, correct, or request deletion of your personal data. You may also request restriction of processing or object to certain uses of your data. To exercise these rights, contact our Privacy Officer.

Contact for Privacy Concerns

For any privacy-related questions or requests, please contact our Privacy Officer at privacy@omanfinancial.com.
